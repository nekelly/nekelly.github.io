* Welcome to "The True Vine" Apologetics


John 15:1 "I am the True Vine"


This purpose of this website is to present arguments in support of the following claims:

  • Atheism is irrational and self-defeating
  • Belief in God is rational
  • Christianity is the only true religion

The arguments are drawn from science (cosmology, physics, biology) and philosophy. These arguments are not intended to be exhaustive but rather a starting point for the interested reader to further explore them in depth. The "books" section gives recommendations for further reading.

- Noel Kelly
